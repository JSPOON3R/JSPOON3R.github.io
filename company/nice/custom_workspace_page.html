Troubleshooting

Web page not working?

Make sure the customer has joined with the correct link. Copy the “Customer Link” from the link menu.

Video not working?

For you: Make sure you have granted permissions for your camera and mic.

For the Customer: If the Customer is having problems with their inputs, instruct them to join the session from their devices browser (instead of within another app).

Copy the "Link with Instructions". to the customer to help them.
